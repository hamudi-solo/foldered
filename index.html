The Hidden forest !!!

A journey beyond the myths

Everyone in the village knew the stories: the forest beyond the northern ridge was cursed. They said no one who entered ever returned, that strange lights floated between the trees at night, and whispers followed those who came too close. Children were warned never to wander near, and most adults pretended it didn’t exist at all.

For in the heart of the forest lay a clearing where an ancient tree stood — its bark etched with symbols glowing softly in the twilight. Elia felt a warm pulse from the tree, as if it were alive and breathing. She reached out, placing her hand on its trunk, and a rush of memories flooded her mind — visions of people who once protected the forest, of forgotten magic, and a promise that one day, a brave soul would come to restore its light.

Elia knew then that her journey was only beginning, and the forest had chosen her to be its new guardian.

Key Takeaways from the Story:

  • The forest beyond the village was feared for generations, believed to be cursed and filled with danger.
  • Elia, a curious and fearless girl, refused to accept the stories as truth.
  • Guided by her own sense of wonder, she entered the forest alone at sunrise.
  • Inside, she discovered a world unlike anything told in legends: glowing trees, magical creatures, and a peaceful, living environment.
  • The forest responded to her presence, as if it had been expecting her all along.
  • At the heart of the forest, Elia found an ancient, enchanted tree filled with memories of the past.
  • The visions revealed that the forest was once protected by guardians — and now, that role might belong to Elia.
  • Her journey was no accident. The forest had chosen her, and a new chapter was about to begin.
